# My 12-Week Training Program

**5 days/week · 6 exercises per day · 3 phases**

---

## About Me

- 43 years old, training 10+ years, lean and fit
- Mind-muscle connection approach — slow, precise, controlled movements
- Left side injury history: scapula inflammation -> cortisone injection -> pain resolved but left side performance dropped drastically
- Goal: rebalance left-right asymmetry, maintain/grow chest and arms, maintain abs
- NOT a bodybuilder, but maintaining good definition

---

## Program Goals

1. **Left-side rehab** — unilateral exercises throughout, start LEFT, match on right, don't compensate
2. **Big chest** — chest 2x/week (Day 2 + Day 5)
3. **Big arms** — triceps 2x/week, biceps 2x/week, plus dips
4. **Abs maintenance** — 2x/week (Day 2 abs, Day 3 obliques), enough for someone already lean
5. **Glutes** — single-leg hip thrust on Day 3

---

## Rep Scheme Logic

| Scheme | Purpose | Used for |
|---|---|---|
| **3x10** | Strength-biased hypertrophy — heaviest load | Compounds + unilateral starters when fresh |
| **3x12** | Hypertrophy sweet spot — moderate load, main volume | Primary working sets |
| **2x15** | Metabolic / pump — lighter load, higher reps | Isolation, finishers |

Order within a day: heaviest first -> main volume -> lighter finisher.

---

## Phase Progression

| Phase | Weeks | Tempo | Rest | Focus |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1-4 | 4-1-1 (slow) | 60s | Build pattern, light weight |
| 2 | 5-8 | 3-1-1 | 75s | Add weight |
| 3 | 9-12 | 3-1-1 | 90s | Heavy, drop sets on 2x15 |

---

## Unilateral Protocol

1. Start with **LEFT** (weak side)
2. Do as many reps as left can at RIR 1-2
3. **Match** that rep count on right
4. Don't go heavier on right to compensate

## Key Training Principles

- **Mind-muscle connection** — slow, precise, controlled. No ego lifting.
- **Tempo** — phase-dependent (4-1-1 in P1, 3-1-1 in P2/P3). Eccentric control is key.
- **No warmup exercises in the program** — warmup done separately before starting.
- **6 exercises per day max** — no exceptions.
- **Unilateral = start left** — always. Right matches left's count. Never compensate on right.
- **Abs are flexible** — Day 2 abs exercise rotates based on feel (crunches, leg raises, cable crunch, etc.)
